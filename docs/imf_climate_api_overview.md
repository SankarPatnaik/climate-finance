# IMF Climate Data API Overview

This document summarizes the key metadata available for three IMF Climate Data Portal datasets and links to their API endpoints for programmatic access.

## Quarterly Greenhouse Gas (GHG) Air Emissions Accounts
- **Category:** Greenhouse Gas (GHG) Emissions
- **Indicator group:** GHG Emissions Accounts
- **Description:** Quarterly greenhouse gas emissions by activity and by region.
- **Geographic coverage:** Regional
- **Time coverage:** 2010–2024
- **API endpoint:** `https://climatedata.imf.org/datasets/543872e1d86c49e3a3bdf38f2b758f92/api`
- **Notes/Disclaimers:** Estimates are experimental and compilation methods are still in development; users should review available documentation and metadata.

## Annual Greenhouse Gas (GHG) Air Emissions Accounts
- **Category:** Greenhouse Gas (GHG) Emissions
- **Indicator group:** GHG Emissions Accounts
- **Description:** Annual greenhouse gas emissions by activity and by region.
- **Geographic coverage:** Regional
- **Time coverage:** 2010–2023
- **API endpoint:** `https://climatedata.imf.org/datasets/c8579761f19740dfbe4418b205654ddf/api`
- **Notes/Disclaimers:** Estimates are experimental; users are encouraged to consult documentation and metadata for suitability.

## National GHG Emissions Inventories and Mitigation Targets
- **Category:** Greenhouse Gas (GHG) Emissions
- **Indicator group:** National Inventories and Targets
- **Description:** National GHG emissions inventories by sector of origin and nationally determined mitigation targets.
- **Geographic coverage:** 213 countries/economies
- **Time coverage:** 1970–2030
- **API endpoint:** `https://climatedata.imf.org/datasets/72e94bc71f4441d29710a9bea4d35f1d/api`
- **Notes/Disclaimers:** Estimates (e.g., business-as-usual projections and emissions targets) are experimental; methods remain under development, so users should review accompanying documentation and metadata.
